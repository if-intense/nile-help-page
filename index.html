How to Use This Service

1. REGISTER
  • Select your role (Viewer, Exhibitor, Negotiator)
  • Put in your e-mail and create a password (at least 8 characters and 1 uppercase letter) and hit the Submit button.
  • If you register as a Viewer, you can view without verifications, but you will be unable to use all functions.
  • If you are registering as either Negotiator or Exhibitor, you will need to register your e-mail, create a password (at least 8 characters and 1 uppercase letter) and also need to input the company information.
  • Verification email will be sent. Make sure to confirm the email.
2. BROWSE
  • You can browse product/company/article posts from the navigation panel.
3. VIEW/MEET
  • By browsing the posts, you can add articles to your favorites, as the will indicate that you have added.
  • By reading the post (articles, product, company), you can request a "meet" by clicking the REQUEST button.
  • The Offer pop-up with a message will be displayed.
4. MATCH
  • You can either OFFER or FORCE a meet. If you choose to OFFER, you will use one NILE ticket, and if you want to FORCE a match, you will use 3 tickets.
  • The company will be notified of your request, and when the match is made, you will be able to chat and be able to do a business with the company.
5. CHAT
  • After the company accepts your request, you can chat about business deals, products, etc from Chat function on the dashboard.
  • You can use emojis and also message stickers in the chat function.
